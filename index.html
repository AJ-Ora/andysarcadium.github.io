About me

I am a programmer from Finland, born in 1996. I am a student at Kajaani University of Applied Sciences.

I have lots of versatile skills and experience when it comes to all kinds of aspects of software development: both frontend / user interface development and the more hidden backend development are familiar to me.

My focus is on game development, as it offers a challenging (yet rewarding) environment to push the software and entertainment industries to new heights. I am also a strong believer of the potential of virtual / augmented reality (VR/AR) development.

I am a supporter of open-source software development.
